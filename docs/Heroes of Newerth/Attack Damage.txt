There are a few components to a unit's attack damage. 

Primary Damage:
---------------
-Base Damage: This is the damage provided by your hero damage dice as well as damaged given from your primary attribute.

-Bonus Damage: This is the bonus you get from various items and abilities, like buriza gives +75, this would be placed here.

Secondary Damage:
------------------
-Bonus Damage: This is the bonus damage you get from things like MKB bonus damage proc.
