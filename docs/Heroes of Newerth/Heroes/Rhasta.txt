Ability 1:
----------
Targets an enemy unit. Releases a lightning that goes to many enemy units dealing magical damage.


Ability 2:
----------
Targets an enemy unit, transforms him into a critter. This changes your base movement speed to the critters movement speed. This interrupts channeling abilities. While a critter, you cannot cast spells or attack.


Ability 3:
----------
Targets an enemy unit and binds him. This is a channeling spell. The target cannot move, cast or attack (including items). Deals damage over time. 


Ultimate:
----------
Summons many magic immune 'wards' that deal damage.



Intelligence/Ranged
