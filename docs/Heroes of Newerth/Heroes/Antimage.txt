Ability 1:
----------
This is a passive. It is an attack modifier. It burns mana from the target on each attack. This deals damage bonus magical damage based on a factor of how much mana was drained. If the target has no mana, you drain zero mana, so you deal zero bonus magical damage.


Ability 2:
----------
Target a point on the map. If cast point is beyond the maximum blink, you will blink in that direction. This is a teleportation ability. See teleportation mechanics for more details.


Ability 3:
----------
This is a passive. It gives you bonus magical armor. 


Ultimate:
----------
Targets an enemy unit with this spell, dealing magical damage to him based on how much mana he is missing. This interrupts channeling spells.



Agility/Melee
