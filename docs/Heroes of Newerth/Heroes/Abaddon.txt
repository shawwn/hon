Ability 1: Aphotic Shield
Description: Creates a shield on target allied unit. It will absorb a certain amount of damage before exploding and releasing that damage to enemy units.

Ability 2: Frostmourne
Description: When he attacks an enemy unit with his sword,  he inflicts it with a magical debuff. The target will be slowed briefly and any unit that attacks it will move and attack faster.

Ability 3: Death Coil
Description: At the cost of some of his life force, he releases a dark energy on target enemy unit, damaging it.

Ultimate: Borrowed Time
Description: When abaddon drops below a certain hitpoint level, he will remove all negative buffs on him and will heal instead of taking damage for the duration of this buff.
