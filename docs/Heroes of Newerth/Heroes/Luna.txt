Ability 1:
----------
Ranged Spell, single enemy target. Deals magic damage and interrupts channeling spells.


Ability 2:
----------
Passive. Makes your attack bounce from target to target, dealing reduced damage with each successive bounce.


Ability 3:
----------
Aura. Increases base damage of ranged allies.


Ultimate:
----------
At short intervals, releases nukes (same as first ability) targetting enemy units at random
