Ability 1:
----------
Target an enemy unit, adds a slow and reduces target's armor. Whenever u cast this on the enemy, if they still have the buff, it resets the duration and increases the effect.


Ability 2:
----------
Deals physical damage in an area around you. A unit hit by this will take extra damage each other time it gets hit by this for a limited amount of time.


Ability 3:
----------
This reduces damage you take from your rear. Whenever you take damage on your rear, it counts it until a certain amount and casts an instances of ability 2.


Ultimate:
----------
Whenever you cast a spell, you gain bonus movement and attack speed. You start off with a base bonus and then an additional bonus for each subsequent cast.
