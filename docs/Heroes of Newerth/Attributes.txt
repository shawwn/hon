Each attribute gives passive bonuses. 

1 Strength gives 19 Hit Points
1 Strength gives 0.05 Health Regeneration
1 Intelligence gives 13 Mana Points
1 Intelligence gives 0.04 Mana Regeneration
1 Agility gives 0.14 Physical Armor 
1 Agility gives 1 Attack Speed


These are added to the base properties.
